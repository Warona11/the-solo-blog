Origins of Tension* DJ Maphorisa and Samthing Soweto were both part of the massive success of the *Amapiano* genre, particularly with hits like "Amantombazane" and "Akulaleki." Fans Fuel the Drama Fans of both artists have often fueled the fire, pitting the two against each other on social media, comparing their contributions, and amplifying the narrative of a rift.
